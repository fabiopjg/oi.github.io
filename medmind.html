How the meditation can help you and your health...

Nowadays meditation is so important for our lives. We are so engaged in the digital world that we have forgotten our health and mind.
In your day, I'm pretty sure that you have five minutes to practice this method, you can find in your schedule a time to relax and don't do anything. Just breathe and be fluid...

Why meditation is a good practice?

Well, I have meditated almost every day, I'm trying to change this "almost" for a "habit". Meditation is to have a connection with yourself and organize your feelings and thinkings. Before, I believed meditation only to sleep, but this year I started meditating again and I have to learn more and more about its rewards.
